Developed for the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) on Republic Day, this project redefined traditional print storytelling by merging national pride with digital innovation. The campaign transformed a standard newspaper advertisement into a multi sensory, interactive WebAR experience centered around a unique musical rendition of "Saare Jahan Se Achha". Every single beat of the track was meticulously composed using the actual sound design of digital payment notification jingles..

The visual narrative mapped out the widespread impact of NPCI’s ecosystem across the nation. Scanning the printed page unlocked an immersive, animated 3D layer that brought the newspaper to life, telling the story of the Indian flag's journey. The sequence tracked the flag from its initial manufacturing in a warehouse, to shipping logistics, to enterprise setups, to small retail shops, and finally to a heartfelt celebration by school children. By closing the loop between physical print, digital interaction, and a custom audio soundscape, the installation beautifully honored India's parallel strides in national progress and technological infrastructure.
