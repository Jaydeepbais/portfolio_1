About Me

I am an Experience Designer with over 8 years of expertise in bridging physical and digital worlds through immersive technology, spatial design, and cutting edge visual mediums. Grounded in a Bachelor of Design in Communication Design, my career has been dedicated to transforming complex data, stories, and brand narratives into captivating, interactive spaces.

My design practice lives at the intersection of human interaction and advanced technology. I specialize in the architecture and execution of interactive installations, projection mapping, AR/VR/MR applications, anamorphic video experiences, and custom AI production workflows. Backing this creative vision is a robust technical skill set utilizing real-time rendering and physical computing engines, including Unreal Engine, Unity, TouchDesigner, Notch, and ComfyUI.

My systems have been deployed for market-leading brands, global broadcasters, and institutional museums. My portfolio features high impact experiential work for the National Archives Museum, NPCI, Spotify, Siemens, BharatBenz, and Jaguar, Gulf, Adhiraj Constructions, Limelight alongside advanced broadcasting solutions for IPL 2026. Driven by material curiosity and technological innovation, I design environments that do more than engage an audience they leave a lasting imprint.
