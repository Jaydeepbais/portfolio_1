Studio

We are a technology-driven art and design studio operating at the intersection of media arts, neuroscience, architecture, and machine intelligence.

Founded in 2014, our practice centers on using data as pigment — transforming vast datasets into immersive sensory experiences that reveal hidden patterns in the world around us.

From permanent public installations to ephemeral performances, we explore how machine learning can be used not as a tool of automation, but as a medium for human expression and collective memory.
