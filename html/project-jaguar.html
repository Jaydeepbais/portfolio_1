Concept

Developed under Transhuman Collective for the virtual launch of the all electric Jaguar I‑PACE, this project featured the creation of Futuropolis—a fully realized, high-fidelity smart city built entirely within Unreal Engine. The digital universe was meticulously engineered to showcase intelligent architecture, cutting-edge technology, and sustainable mobility solutions, positioning electric vehicles at the very center of tomorrow’s urban landscape. By leveraging real-time 3D environments, the virtual launch seamlessly integrated the vehicle’s premium design with futuristic world-building, delivering an immersive, forward-thinking narrative experience that redefined the boundaries of automotive digital product launches.

As the lead technical artist and environmental designer, I oversaw the visual production from initial world-building through to final post-production. Made the comprehensive architectural layout and asset design of the Futuropolis metropolis, crafting a sprawling, futuristic urban ecosystem optimized within Unreal Engine. Within this digital environment, I animated the high octane car cinematics, perfectly capturing the vehicle's dynamics, form, and premium aesthetic. Additionally, I managed the integration of real-world elements by leading the green screen shoot for the presenters and handled the intensive post-production pipeline, seamlessly blending live action footage with real-time 3D environments to deliver a polished, cinematic launch film.
