Concept

To design a striking, high-impact visual centerpiece for the launch of Limelight’s flagship store in Mumbai. As a pioneering CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) lab grown diamond company, the brand required an installation that could instantly communicate their unique intersection of luxury, scientific innovation, and environmental consciousness.

In colaboartion we Arthat Studio we developed an immersive anamorphic LED installation designed to captivate audiences through dynamic, forced-perspective illusions. The core visual narrative was built around the fascinating genesis of a lab-grown diamond. Through elegant, high-fidelity CGI storytelling, the piece visually translated the complex laboratory process of creating a diamond from a single seed. It then seamlessly transitioned into a broader narrative about sustainability, highlighting the positive environmental impact of lab-grown gems compared to traditional mining.

By fusing modern, luxurious design aesthetics with advanced spatial graphics, the installation served as a magnetic focal point for the store launch. The anamorphic animations broke the boundaries of the flat digital screen, creating a 3D optical illusion that reached out to the audience. The final piece did more than just decorate the space, it educated, engaged, and perfectly embodied the brilliance and forward-thinking ethos of the Limelight brand.
