Concept

Designed for the grand unveiling of Hyderabad’s Greenfield Airport logo, this high-impact anamorphic installation blended regional identity with global ambition by drawing visual inspiration from the Palapitta, the iconic state bird of Telangana. The immersive, forced perspective narrative celebrated the airport's emergence as a premier international aviation hub, creatively weaving its key operational milestones including a network connecting over 80 global destinations, a centralized Airport Operation Control Center, and a ranking of 2nd globally in On Time Performance (OTP) into a seamless 3D optical illusion. By uniting cultural symbolism with modern, data driven achievements, the final anamorphic display served as a powerful, larger than life centerpiece for the launch event, transforming operational excellence into a captivating visual spectacle.
